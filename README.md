# connect-four 
Connect four game in the command line 



## Example Game
``` powershell
...
O's Turn
Choose Collumn: 3

_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ X O _ _ _
O X X X _ _ _
X O O O _ _ _

0 1 2 3 4 5 6

X's turn
Choose Collumn: 3
X wins!

_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ _ _ _ _ _
_ _ _ X _ _ _
_ _ X O _ _ _
O X X X _ _ _
X O O O _ _ _

0 1 2 3 4 5 6
```
